Tareq Abuissa is a New York-based composer, lyricist, and music director. He works for the Society of Composers & Lyricists, and serves as a Teaching Artist for Very Young Composers with the New York Philharmonic. His four stage musicals include the tech industry satire South of Market: The Musical (2016, 2017) and a new musical Naked (2020), where one of Manet's nude models flees his painting in a museum. While studying Music and Mathematics at Columbia University, Abuissa co-composed the 118th Varsity Show, The Corporate Core (2012).

Abuissa is the songwriter of his indie band's debut album Ruby Jubilee (2018). His scores for the screen include three USC Animation thesis films (2014) and two video interview series produced by Lincoln Center (2013).
